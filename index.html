Welcome to Wolfpack Neurotech!

Wolfpack Neurotech is a student run organization committed to spreading education and building meaningful projects at the intersection of neuroscience, biomedical engineering, electrical engineering, machine learning, materials science, and more. We aim to create a community of students who are passionate about applying technology to improve the lives of those around them. Further, we hope to serve as a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and build innovative projects that interface with the brain and nervous system.
